        <h1 id="wb-cont" property="name">Restaurant and food service inspection in Canada</h1>
<!-- WMCS:GDR-NOTICE --><!-- WCMS:NOTICE --><p>Do you have concerns about food you have eaten in a restaurant? Restaurant and food service inspection across Canada is generally carried out by these organizations:</p>

<ul>
<li>provincial governments</li>
<li>municipalities</li>
<li>regional health authorities</li>
</ul>

<p>They inspect facilities to verify that safe food-handling practices are being followed. You can report concerns about restaurant food to your local public health department.</p>

<p>If you suspect that your health has been compromised because of food, contact your doctor or local public health centre, who will follow up with the appropriate treatment.</p>

<h3>The CFIA's Role</h3>
